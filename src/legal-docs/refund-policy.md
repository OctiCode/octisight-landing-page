# Refund Policy

**Effective date:** 26 May 2026
**Version:** 1.0

This Refund Policy explains when OctiSight offers refunds for paid subscriptions. It forms part of the [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md).

The free trial does not involve payment and is not subject to this policy.

---

## 1. The 14-day money-back guarantee

OctiSight offers a **14-day money-back guarantee** on your **first paid subscription**.

If you are not satisfied with the Service, you may request a full refund within **fourteen (14) days** of your initial paid invoice. We will refund the amount paid, less any non-refundable third-party fees (e.g., currency conversion fees imposed by your bank or payment processor) where they apply.

### 1.1 Eligibility

To be eligible for the money-back guarantee:

1. You must request the refund **within 14 days** of the date of your first paid invoice.
2. The request must concern your **first paid subscription** with OctiSight. Renewals and upgrades after the initial 14-day window are not covered (see Sections 2 and 3).
3. Your account must be in good standing, no outstanding violations of the [Acceptable Use Policy](./acceptable-use-policy.md).

### 1.2 How to request

Email **`support@octisight.com`** from the email address on your account, with the subject line "Refund request" and your organisation name. We will acknowledge within three (3) business days and process eligible refunds within ten (10) business days to the original payment method via Stripe.

---

## 2. Renewals

By default, paid subscriptions **auto-renew** at the end of each billing period.

- **Monthly subscriptions** renew monthly.
- **Annual subscriptions** renew annually.

If you do not want your subscription to renew, **cancel before the renewal date** in your in-app billing settings or by emailing `support@octisight.com`. Cancellations take effect at the end of the then-current billing period you retain access until then.

OctiSight does **not** refund charges for a renewed billing period that you forgot to cancel, **except**:

- where required by applicable consumer protection law (see Section 4);
- as a goodwill gesture at our discretion, particularly where the renewal is genuinely accidental and you have not actively used the Service in that period.

For annual renewals, we send a reminder email at least **fourteen (14) days** before the renewal date so you have time to act.

---

## 3. Upgrades, downgrades, and mid-cycle changes

- **Upgrades** are billed pro-rata for the remainder of the current billing period. The upgrade takes effect immediately.
- **Downgrades** take effect at the end of the current billing period. We do not refund the difference between the old and new tier for the unused remainder of the current period, except where required by law or agreed in your Order Form.
- **Seat increases / decreases** follow the same pattern: increases pro-rated, decreases at next renewal.

---

## 4. EU and UK consumer right of withdrawal

If you are a **consumer** resident in the EU, EEA, or United Kingdom, you have a statutory right to withdraw from the contract within **14 days** of conclusion under EU Directive 2011/83/EU (Consumer Rights Directive) and the UK Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013.

OctiSight is a B2B service intended for business use, so most users will not qualify as consumers. If you do, this right is in addition to the 14-day money-back guarantee.

### 4.1 Loss of withdrawal right for digital services

By starting to use the Service during the 14-day period, you agree that the supply of the digital service has begun, and you acknowledge that you may **lose your right of withdrawal** to the extent the service has been performed. Even where the statutory right is lost, the 14-day money-back guarantee in Section 1 still applies.

---

## 5. Cancellations for cause

If OctiSight **materially breaches** the [Terms of Service](./terms-of-service.md) and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of written notice, you may terminate the affected subscription and receive a **pro-rata refund** of pre-paid fees for the unused remainder of the then-current term.

If OctiSight terminates your account **for cause** (e.g., for breach of the Acceptable Use Policy, non-payment after notice, illegality), **no refund is due**.

---

## 6. Enterprise contracts

For customers on an Enterprise Agreement or custom Order Form, the refund terms in the contract control. The 14-day money-back guarantee in Section 1 does not automatically apply to Enterprise contracts unless explicitly included.

---

## 7. Refund of taxes

Where a refund is issued, applicable VAT, TVA, or sales tax is refunded along with the fee. Where the original tax was remitted to a tax authority, our processing time may be slightly longer.

---

## 8. Chargebacks

Please contact us at `support@octisight.com` **before** initiating a chargeback. We can almost always resolve billing disputes faster and more easily than your bank can. Initiating a chargeback while a refund request is open with us may result in the suspension of your account pending resolution.

---

## 9. Currency

Refunds are issued in the **currency originally charged**. We do not compensate for exchange rate fluctuations between the date of the original charge and the date of the refund.

---

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The **terms in effect on the date of your invoice** apply to that invoice. Updates apply only to invoices issued after the effective date of the change.
